SHORT HISTORY

If the first models were born in the 18th century, it is only at the end of the 19th century that the first real cars were put on the road. The automobile became a luxury object that people wanted to exhibit. It is in the Tuileries Park, in France, in 1898 that the big car shows were born. Only cars that could travel the distance Paris-Versailles-Paris were accepted in the show. After the First World War, mass-produced cars gradually became available to a wider public. In the 1930s, automobile tourism expanded. In the United States, thousands of families went into debt to buy a car. In the 1950s, very cheap models were produced and sold by the thousands.Today, the car represents the status of the individual who owns it.
